About Project

The Digital Wellness Monitoring Platform is an IoT-based system that helps users track their health and wellness. It monitors heart rate, activity, sleep, hydration, screen time and generates a wellness score. The platform provides alerts, reports and personalized recommendations to encourage healthy lifestyle habits.
